Constitution

Rules are very necessary things. They help to keep safe and organized. It is important to follow all the rules, such as laws, regulations and policies. If one breaks the rules, he will get punished by the government.

In this page, we will know about the rules or laws or regulations of TechnoBladersND SMP , in short TBND.

All the members as well as admins or moderators should obey the rules of SMP.

All these rules are imposed by the TechnoBladers Main Townhall Prime , especilly Goverment of TB.

There are 3 major Sections(clauses) in which all the rules or act(s) are divided:-

  1. Mining Clause
  2. Legal Clause
  3. Development Clause

Mining Clause

In this 'Mining Clause', includes:-

NRCR Act :-Full form of this act is Natural Resources Collecting and Reservatives Act. It says 3 rules:-

1) Mining should be only done in permissionable areas.

2) If mining is done under the townhall then the government will punish the miner.

3) In some places that could happen soil slide, mining is restricted.

Legal Clause

In this clause, there are many rules that everyone has to follow.
